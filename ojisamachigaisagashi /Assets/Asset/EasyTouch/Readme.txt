Easy Touch V1.0:
================

Allows you to quickly and easily develop actions based on a touchscreen or a mouse (including a simulation for the second finger).
All major gestures are recognized by EasyTouch such as tap, double tap, swipe, twist, pinch ... and automatic selection of objects located under the touches.

And much more...

You don't need prefabs or complex setup, just the EasyTouch library and implement the methods called by EasyTouch.
Develop for mobile platforms has never been easier.

With the complete simulation of the second finger, quietly developing in Unity, without having to go through the step of loading the emulator or on the regular target mobile solution.
If it works with the mouse, it will work with a touchscreen

The package contains the library, examples and documentation.

Look at the documentation directory for more informations.
